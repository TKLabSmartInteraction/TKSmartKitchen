TK SmartKitchen

Implementation of the SmartKitchen

The SmartKitchen is a project of the project practikum in the summer semester at TK 2012. The goal is to build up a part of a kitchen and attach different kinds of sensors to track what users are doing and later to support the user with his objectives.
